Share mobile phone memory

Allocating 1GB (of unused memory) to DataHop you can store and distribute a whole 1h movie. The aggregate storage capacity at the edge of the network (i.e., in users' mobile and WiFi devices) is more than the storage capacity of a server farm - we just need to connect the right dots at the right time.

Exchange content of your interest

Effectively you store-carry-and-forward content and act as a data mule as you move around the city. There are hundreds of transfer opportunities in places such as trains, restaurants, coffee shops and workplaces. You’re not just connected to the network - you can be part of the network.

Get compensated

The more you move in crowded areas and the more memory you have contributed to the system, the more content you share with others transparently. In turn, the reward you receive for your contribution to the system increases accordingly.

Features

Energy consumption kept to a minimum

Our sophisticated D2D connectivity module guarantees that you won’t spend more than 10% per 12hrs of participating in the system.

No privacy or DRM violations

All content is encrypted and you can only watch content you are entitled to, i.e., based on subscription and DRM policies. Transfers happen between anonymised node IDs - we don’t care who you transferred content to, only how many transfers you did.

Get your content even without connecvitiy

You will have access to the content of your interest even if connectivity is not available, without consuming your mobile data.

The technology

Energy efficient Information-centric Connectivity

DataHop devices connect only when they have content to transfer to each other and not randomly to any device in the vicinity. This is an important feature which improves performance significantly, but also keeps energy consumption at low levels. The content-centric approach to D2D connectivity is a unique feature of DataHop.

Secure and Private D2D Interactions

DataHop keeps communications secure and private. It provides full integrity of the content shared avoiding any malicious users to spread any virus or poisoned content. Also, full confidentiality can be provided for that content that only a set of users should have access to it. Users are authenticated periodically in order to avoid any impersonation attack and no private information can be leaked from the D2D communications. Also, our distibuted platform is robust to any DoS attack giving full availability to users.
