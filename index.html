How it works?

The technology

Smart D2D Connectivity

DataHop utilises direct Device to Device (D2D) connectivity between smartphones to distribute content locally, without requiring Internet access.

Our Content-Centric D2D Connectivity solution initiates secure connections only when new content is available in the vicinity, reducing overall battery consumption.

Token-based reward system

DataHop rewards users that participate in the DataHop network through a sophisticated, blockchain-based Proof-of-Transfer consensus algorithm.

As nodes move, they naturally form distribution trees of content transfers. The longer the distribution tree, the more the reward that users are entitled to.
